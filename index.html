Octopus
my lonely friend
omni-existence till the end
a mirrored reflection
of your world

No shape too crazy
camouflage color never lazy

iron becomes cupper
and
blood becomes aristocratic
and

Octopus
You love your friends
yet you are lonesome.
Curiosity and Cautiousness
two ever fighting wishes

attracted to what
you escape from
Only in your dreams
you forget your fear

And your colors
show your deepest self
but careful little octopus,
to Dare to Dream.
